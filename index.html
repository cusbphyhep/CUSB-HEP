Welcome to the High Energy Physics Lab

This site provides resources and information about nuclear and particle physics conducted at Central University South Bihar (CUSB).

HEP-Detector Development Lab

Central University of South Bihar

Advanced research in High Energy Physics detector development, focusing on cutting-edge neutrino detection, dark matter research, and cosmic ray physics.

Research Areas

🔬

Neutrino Physics

Advanced neutrino detection, oscillation studies, and sterile neutrino searches using cutting-edge detector technologies.

NOvA collaboration DUNE R&D Texono experiment
🌌

Dark Matter Research

Direct detection methods, underground experiments, and novel detector development for WIMP and axion searches.

Pire-GAMEDARK Xenon detector R&D Background studies
🌌

Cosmic Ray and Space Physics

Studies on high-energy cosmic rays and their radio emissions from extensive air showers, along with investigations of solar–terrestrial interactions, magnetospheric dynamics, and space weather effects on Earth’s environment. Includes observational astronomy for understanding solar and cosmic phenomena.

Radio Emission from EAS Space Weather & Magnetosphere Solar–Terrestrial Physics Observational Astronomy
🔧

Detector Development

Next-generation detector technology, electronics development, and novel readout systems for HEP experiments.

Scintilator Detectors development Front-End Readout System design Gaseous Detectors
